# MedBot - The Medical Chatbot  
  
## Introduction  
  
MedBot is a capstone project being undertaken by a team of Columbia University students at the [Data Science Institute](https://datascience.columbia.edu). The team consists of Andrew Satz, Jonathan Galsurkar, Minghong Zheng, Shengyang Zhang and Janak A Jain.  
  
---  

## About the Project  
  
The project is sponsored by [Synergic Partners](http://www.synergicpartners.com/en/), A Telefonica company and involves creation of a chatbot service that can communicate with a user and provide informed medical advice related to medicinal and drug prescription. The chatbot would encapsulate capabilities involving natural language processing, artifical intellgience and machine learning.
